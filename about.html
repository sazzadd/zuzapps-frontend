Our Mission

Built For The Teams Who Refuse To Settle For Ordinary Growth.

We believe businesses can grow with a conscience and succeed with a soul and they can do it with the help of software that puts the customer first. That's why we build Zuapps: a CRM platform that connects sales, service, marketing, and operations around a single, honest view of every customer.

Our customers grow faster than the market average. They retain more revenue. They make decisions with confidence. That's the work and we measure ourselves against it every quarter.

Our Story

Two founders, one stubborn idea.

Zuapps started in a Lisbon apartment in 2019, when Aria and Mateo got tired of watching their friends in sales wrestle with software that treated them like data entry clerks. They believed the relationship between a company and its customer deserved better tools — calmer, kinder, more honest.

Six years on, that conviction hasn't moved. We're now 320+ people across 14 offices, but every roadmap decision still starts the same way: with a real customer story, not a sales target.
